    Hearts of Art Welcomes You!

    DEUS EX PANDEMUS: THE DISCUSSION

    'Deus Ex Pandemus' literally translates to 'God In A Pandemic'. Like the name suggests, this conference will explore the positive aspects and blessings in disguise that have come about due to the pandemic.

    2020.6.27 (SAT)

    @ Kenya National Theatre

    Main Program

    Play

    A play with interesting twists and turns focused on the lives of different people during the pandemic.

    Presentations

    Various presentations by artists and researchers relating to the pandemic and related subjects

    Panel

    An open panel discussing the impact of the pandemic on our society and what can be done about it.

    SEE THE WHOLE PROGRAM

    Featured Speakers

    Walter Sitati

    Founder and President of Hearts of Art Theatre Group

    alter Sitati has had an accomplished career as a communications consultant, actor, director, writer, and producer. At Hearts of Art, he often casts a critical but humorous lens on modern day society, expressing the zeitgeist through the language of art.

    Patience Akinyi

    Actress and Sales & Marketing Consultant

    Patience Akinyi has had first-hand experience of the impact of the pandemic on the service sector, and has done extensive research into different approaches to adapt the industry to the changing times. She also has a passion for acting.

    Azziad Nasenya

    Actress and Social Media Influencer

    The story of Azziad Nasenya's rise to fame is as tragic as it is encouraging, and her stardom is partly influenced, if not entirely caused by, the changing moods of Kenyans on social media as the pandemic takes hold. She has deep insight into the shifting attitudes of Kenyans online, as well as the different ways they are dealing with the pandemic.

    Elvis Gatere

    Actor and Molecular Biologies

    Elvis Gatere's career is a crossroads between perhaps the unlikeliest of fields: acting and molecular biology. One of these fields, however, has given him a deep insight into the workings of the pandemic, and the other allows him to communicate them in an accessible way.

    Tracy Amadi

    Actress and Nutrionist

    Tracy Amadi explores the impact of the pandemic on our eating habits, both positive and negative. She also offers valuable advice on how to stay healthy and boost our immunity in these trying times.

    Peter Kawa

    Founder and CEO of Sanaapost

    Sanaapost is an online directory of various talents in the entertainment industry, and has taken a frontline position in raising awareness about the pandemic. Peter Kawa talks about its efforts, successes, and struggles.
