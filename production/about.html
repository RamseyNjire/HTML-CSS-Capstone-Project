DEUS EX PANDEMUS

THE DISCUSSION

header

'Deus Ex Pandemus' is a Latin phrase which literally translates to 'God in a Pandemic'.

This is a play on the famous phrase Deus Ex Machina and is meant to express
the possibility that this pandemic might actually be a blessing in disguise.
Through the media of theater, presentation, and panel discussions, this program
seeks to explore just what these blessings might be.

Please contact us on email for any further questions about the Deus Ex Pandemus event or Hearts of Art.
